# Imported Asset Packs

Imported legal CC0 packs for the 2D platformer:

- `Assets/Art/Kenney/NewPlatformerPack`
  - Tiles, backgrounds, props, enemies, collectibles, spritesheets, and source vector files.
- `Assets/Art/UI/Kenney`
  - HUD and menu art, UI font assets, and source vector files.
- `Assets/Art/Sprites/Player/Kenney`
  - Player pose sprites and modular limb parts.
- `Assets/Art/InputPrompts/Kenney`
  - Keyboard, mouse, gamepad, generic prompts, flairs, fonts, vectors, and spritesheets for control hints.
- `Assets/Art/Kenney/PlatformerIndustrial`
  - Extra industrial platformer tiles, spritesheets, and environment art that can be used for pipe-like and mechanical level pieces.
- `Assets/Art/Kenney/BackgroundElements`
  - Extra parallax-friendly background art, spritesheets, and vectors for sky and layered scene depth.
- `Assets/Art/Kenney/PlatformerExtendedEnemies`
  - Additional enemy sprites and alien/enemy variants for more gameplay variety.
- `Assets/Art/Kenney/RoguelikeRpgPack`
  - Extra dungeon-style props and item art that can supply treasure chest, key, and relic-style pickups.
- `Assets/Art/Kenney/TopDownShooter`
  - Gun sprites, shooter characters, tiles, spritesheets, and source vector files.
- `Assets/Art/Effects/Kenney/ParticlePack`
  - Fire, smoke, spark, and particle textures for muzzle flashes, impacts, and projectile effects.
- `Assets/Audio/SFX/Kenney/NewPlatformerPack`
  - Extra platformer sound effects bundled with the art pack.
- `Assets/Audio/SFX/Kenney/ImpactSounds`
  - Gameplay impact and hit sounds.
- `Assets/Audio/SFX/Kenney/InterfaceSounds`
  - Menu and interaction sounds.
- `Assets/Audio/SFX/Kenney/UIAudio`
  - UI-focused sound effects.
- `Assets/Audio/Music/Kenney/MusicJingles`
  - Short music cues and jingles.

Import rules:

- Textures in imported Kenney art folders use `Point` filtering.
- Texture compression is disabled.
- Spritesheets in `Spritesheets` import as `Multiple`.
- Other sprite textures import as `Single`.
- Imported audio uses uncompressed `PCM`.
